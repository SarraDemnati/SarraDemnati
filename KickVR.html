KickVR

Get ready for an exciting VR adventure in a magical world where a 3D printed shoe holds a special connection. Your goal in this game is to play through a series of games that not only provide incredible gameplay but also unveil the secrets of the shoe's transformation powers. Each game offers a unique experience and unlocks new possibilities for shaping and modifying the shoe. From scaling towering cliffs to planting enchanting mushrooms, you'll be immersed in exhilarating challenges that bring out the shoe's full potential.

But the excitement doesn't stop there! After conquering each game and discovering the shoe's amazing abilities, you have the opportunity to bring your virtual creation to life. With the help of a 3D printer, you can save your customized shoe model and hold a tangible masterpiece in your hands. Prepare for an awe-inspiring journey of exploration and discovery. This VR adventure will leave you eagerly anticipating the wonders that await as you unleash your imagination and shape your destiny, one thrilling game at a time.
