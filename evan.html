Evan

In this game, players assume the role of a young orphan who finds themselves trapped within the confines of an ominous orphanage. The primary objective is to navigate through the various rooms and corridors, seeking a means of escape. To accomplish this, players must engage with a variety of puzzles and challenges scattered throughout the orphanage.

These puzzles serve as gateways, requiring players to decipher clues, locate hidden objects, or solve intricate riddles in order to unlock doors and progress further into the building. For instance, one puzzle might entail searching for a concealed key within a specific room, which, once found, can be used to unlock a door leading to the next area.
